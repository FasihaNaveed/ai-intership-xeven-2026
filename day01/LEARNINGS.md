# AI Research Findings

## Research Question

**Explain what Artificial Intelligence is with real-world examples.**

## Comparison Table

| Source         | Key Points                                                                                        | Examples Provided                                              | Clarity Rating |
| -------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------- | -------------- |
| ChatGPT        | AI is the simulation of human intelligence by machines. It can learn, reason, and make decisions. | Voice assistants, recommendation systems, self-driving cars    | 9/10           |
| Gemini         | AI enables computers to perform tasks that normally require human intelligence.                   | Google Maps, Google Translate, smart assistants                | 9/10           |
| Claude         | AI helps computers analyze information, recognize patterns, and solve problems.                   | Email filtering, medical diagnosis support, virtual assistants | 8.5/10         |
| Medium Article | AI is a field of computer science focused on creating intelligent systems.                        | Chatbots, facial recognition, online recommendations           | 8/10           |

## Key Learnings

* AI enables machines to perform tasks that normally require human intelligence.
* AI is already used in many everyday applications.
* Different AI tools explain the same concept with slightly different focus areas.
* AI is becoming important in healthcare, education, business, and technology.
